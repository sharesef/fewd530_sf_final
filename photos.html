From the photographer

"It's been nearly 3 years since I moved to Washington Heights near 181st and Riverside. So much has changed in the areas between 181st and 160th streets, which feels unnerving to me. Since being here, I've taken the time to capture these rapid changes. I often hear that this neighborhood is among the last places in Manhattan considered affordable... Now, as more and more local establishments have closed due to rising prices in rent, maintenance and etc. many people that I meet have the same question: 'Where will we go or is it worth staying?" Even in the Bronx - where I am from people wonder how best to handle these changes."

- Sharese Ann Frederick
