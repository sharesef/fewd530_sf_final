About

Sharese Ann Frederick is a photographer, and a new board member of the Jessie Smith Noyes Foundation that supports grassroots organizations focused on the environment and social justice. She also loves to develop web content for local nonprofits.

As an artist, She primarily focuses street photography, where she often captures moments that are often overlooked by pedestrians such as: general street life, urban decay and local events.
